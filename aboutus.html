ABOUT SEDRICA

SeDriCa 1.0 - The Autonomous Ground Vehicle:

Innovation Cell: Founded by Prof. C. Amarnath, the Innovation Cell aims to foster creativity, innovation, and exploration on the IIT Bombay campus. Over the years, it has evolved from nurturing small hobby projects to solving complex industrial challenges.
SeDriCa’s Achievement: SeDriCa, an electric autonomous ground vehicle developed by the Innovation Cell, secured an outstanding 4th position globally in the Intelligent Ground Vehicle Competition (IGVC) 2016. This achievement is remarkable because it’s the best performance by any Asian team in this prestigious competition1.
Competition Details: The IGVC is an annual international robotics competition for undergraduate and graduate students. It involves navigating a track autonomously while avoiding obstacles, staying within white lanes, and accurately achieving GPS points. SeDriCa excelled in both the Basic Auto-Nav Challenge and the Advanced Auto-Nav Challenge, outperforming teams from top-ranked universities like CMU and Virginia Tech1.
Vehicle Features: SeDriCa is equipped with sensors for environment sensing, including GPS, an IMU (Inertial Measurement Unit), and wheel encoders. The vision system detects lanes, and LiDAR (Light Detection and Ranging) technology identifies static obstacles. Innovative path planning and decision-making algorithms process collected data for successful navigation.
